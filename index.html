Ammunition Components

Cups are the component necessary for the production of cartridge cases. Cups are pressed from brass strips for cases and projectiles and undergo further finishing processes so that they can be best utilised in the manufacture of cases. The first manufacturing process determines the quality of the cases made from these cups.
